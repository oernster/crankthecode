The portfolio contains independent engineering systems and software experiments built outside commercial environments.

These projects are not product demos.

They are applied explorations of the structural ideas described in the Decision Architecture essays.

Each system examines practical concerns such as:

- boundary clarity
- modularity
- authority surfaces
- operational simplicity
- compounding improvement

The principles remain the same.
The medium changes.

This page is a collection of **software systems and engineering experiments**.
